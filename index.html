Mission Brief · §01

The daily signal room for four channels of loose-cannon commentary.

NOD News is a daily desk that pulls the news, filters it through the four modes of the Mix & Match software — HAGGLE, FRIENDSHIP, BOSS MODE, ROGUE — and outputs ready-to-film angles for each. Every morning at 06:00 CT, twelve stories are queued (three per mode) with a source, a headline, and a loose-cannon angle.

House Rules · §03

The rules that keep this monetized.

Every angle has to pass one test: attack ideas, not identities. A CEO's strategy can be a scam. A CEO cannot be called a scammer without a conviction on file. Same rule for celebrities, founders, influencers, and public figures across all four channels.

✅ ALLOWED
Public actions, public statements, public numbers
Transformative commentary on public figures
Profanity, strong emotion, "not financial advice"
❌ BLOCKED
Slurs, hate speech, protected-group attacks
Body-shaming, mental-health speculation
Election / health misinformation
Criminal accusations without a conviction
Content involving minors, ever
